Laboratory of Evolutionary Design

Research

In only a few months, SARS-CoV-2 evolution defeated all of our antibody drugs.
Advances in AI enable biological design capabilities beyond those of natural evolution.
We are focused on building biological AI that is aligned with human health and safety.

Learning from nature

Human immunity is much more resilient than our antibody drugs. Learning how the immune system balances short-term protection and long-term adaptability is critical to designing robust therapeutics.

Accelerating evolution

Evolution is a powerful force, as well as a powerful adversary. By learning evolutionary rules from data, machine learning provides a way to harness this force for our benefit.

Safe biological AI

Current drug design is outcompeted by natural evolution. Generative AI should be directed to helping researchers rapidly deploy countermeasures against harmful biology.
