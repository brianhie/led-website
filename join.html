Joining

Please consider helping us in our mission to develop safe and effective biological AI. We are primarily motivated by the joy of doing bold, interdisciplinary science. If interested in our mission, you can directly join our Stanford lab or collaborate with us through a job at Arc Institute.

Staff scientist @ Stanford

We are recruiting someone to help establish and maintain the experimental research programs in the lab. This person will play an essential role in ensuring that our AI models are safely and effectively translated into real-world impact. If you are interested, please contact us directly with a CV and a short description of your research background.

Infrastructure engineer @ Arc Institute

Arc is recruiting an Infrastructure Engineering Manager to oversee world-class computational infrastructure, which will accelerate our development of cutting-edge biological AI. Please consult this job posting and apply if you are interested.

Postdoctoral associates @ Stanford

We are recruiting postdocs interested in bridging computational and experimental biology, and especially in developing expertise in machine learning or protein biochemistry. Please contact us directly with a CV and a short description of how your career plans align with our mission.

Graduate students @ Stanford

We are especially excited about mentoring the next generation of scientists. If you have been admitted to a graduate program at Stanford University and are interested in conducting your PhD in our lab, please directly send us an email with a CV and a description of a project that you are especially proud of.

Software engineering and machine learning @ Arc Institute

Arc offers an enviroment for biological AI research with access to world-class resources for computation and experimental validation. Please regularly consult the Arc Institute careers page for future job openings in machine learning and software engineering and feel free to contact us directly with any enquiries.
