Joining

Please consider joining our mission to develop safe and effective biological AI. We will also have a lot of fun along the way. If interested, you can directly join our Stanford lab or collaborate with us through a position at Arc Institute.

Positions at Stanford

Postdoctoral fellows

We are recruiting for three postdoc positions. (1) The first is a joint postdoc at Arc Institute with the Hsu Lab focused on integrating synthetic biology with modern machine learning applications. (2) The second is a position in experimental protein engineering, with a focus on applications in immunology and host-pathogen interactions. (3) The third is a position in molecular machine learning, with an ideal candidate having a background in large language models, reinforcement learning, and/or geometric deep learning. Please contact us directly with a CV and a short description of how your career plans align with our mission.

Staff scientist

We are recruiting a scientist to help establish and maintain the lab's experimental wet-bench research programs. If you are interested, please contact us directly with a CV and a short description of your research background.

Graduate students

We are especially excited about mentoring the next generation of scientists. If you have been admitted to a graduate program at Stanford University and are interested in conducting your PhD in our lab, please directly send us an email with a CV and a description of a project that you are especially proud of.
