Joining

Please consider joining our mission to develop safe and effective biological AI. We will also have a lot of fun along the way. If interested, you can directly join our Stanford lab or collaborate with us through a position at Arc Institute.

Positions at Stanford

Staff scientist

We are recruiting a scientist to help establish and maintain the lab's experimental wet-bench research programs. This person will play an essential role in ensuring that our AI models are safely and effectively translated into real-world impact. If you are interested, please contact us directly with a CV and a short description of your research background.

Graduate students

We are especially excited about mentoring the next generation of scientists. If you have been admitted to a graduate program at Stanford University and are interested in conducting your PhD in our lab, please directly send us an email with a CV and a description of a project that you are especially proud of.

Postdoctoral fellows

We are recruiting postdocs interested in bridging computational and experimental biology, especially those wishing to further develop expertise in machine learning or protein biochemistry. Please contact us directly with a CV and a short description of how your career plans align with our mission.

Positions at Arc Institute

Infrastructure engineering manager

Arc is recruiting an Infrastructure Engineering Manager to oversee its computational infrastructure, including the development of large GPU compute capabilities, which will help accelerate our development of biological AI. Please consult this job posting and apply if you are interested.

Software engineering and machine learning

Arc offers an environment for biological AI research with access to world-class resources for computation and experimental validation. Please regularly consult the Arc Institute careers page for future job openings in machine learning and software engineering and feel free to contact us directly with any enquiries.
