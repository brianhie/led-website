Joining

Please consider helping us develop safe and effective AI for biology. Those who find joy from doing science that bridges computational and experimental biology are especially encouraged to join.

If you are interested in a full time role or a postdoc, please see the Institute X careers page. We have openings in experimental protein biochemistry, software engineering, and machine learning.

We are especially excited about mentoring the next generation of scientists. If you have been admitted to a graduate program at Institute X and if you are interested in conducting your PhD in our lab, please directly send an email with a CV and a description of a project that you are particularly proud of.
