Joining

Please consider joining our mission to develop safe and effective biological AI. We will also have a lot of fun along the way.

Postdoctoral fellows and research scientists

We are actively recruiting for two positions at the postdoctoral or research-scientist level at the interface between biology and generative design. Backgrounds of particular interest are (1) in experimental protein biochemistry and protein evolution and (2) in experimental synthetic genomics and synthetic biology. Please contact us directly with a CV, at least two references, and a short description of how your career plans align with our mission.

Graduate students

We are especially excited about mentoring the next generation of scientists. If you have been admitted to a graduate program at Stanford University and are interested in conducting your PhD in our lab, please directly send us an email with a CV and a description of a project that you are especially proud of, preferably before the fall quarter of your matriculation.
